AHEAD OF SCHEDULE & UNDER BUDGET

Originally from England, I have lived and worked in Seattle for over 20 years now. In that time I have been employed as a production artist, graphic designer, art director and general go-to dogsbody.

About

Over the years I have been able to identify and remedy many inefficiencies within the creative — and production — workflows, allowing me to increase production volume without sacrificing creative quality.

If you're looking for someone who won't utter the word ‘synergy’, or try to sell you on ‘re-branding’, but will just stay calm and get the job done, then drop me a line.
